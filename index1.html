About Me

I am a passionate Artist

I’m passionate about being an artist.Art is more than a hobby, it is a burning passion that lights a fire in people's souls. More so, I am very passionate about creativity, using my imagination, being inspired and inspiring others if possible in the process.For being an artist trying to find my way, creativity will always be a winding path. Life is a wandering path sometimes, but creativity will bring you so much more than the normal day to day of almost anything else you could do. Every time you create something you have the unique persecutive of seeing the inner you. There are times when we compare ourselves to others, when we’re unsatisfied with our work, when we question why we’re even doing it to begin with. It’s human nature. But there’s one thing that can keep artists from giving up, from putting away their tools of the trade and succumbing to the average life of those who don’t partake in something that creates discussion, beauty, or personal satisfaction: passion.
